Data Collection Policy for Job Search Help App

1. Introduction

At Job Search Help App, we respect your privacy. This page explains how we handle your personal data when you use our app integrated with LinkedIn's API.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any sensitive personal data unless you provide it directly for the purpose of improving your job search experience.

  • Personal Information: We may collect your name, email address, and job preferences if you provide them.
  • LinkedIn Data: With your consent, we may access some basic public data from your LinkedIn profile such as job titles and skills for providing personalized job recommendations.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for providing job search recommendations and improving our app's functionality.

4. Data Security

We implement standard security measures to protect your information. However, no system is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee full protection.
